PROJECT · DIGITHINGS SERIES · FOUR ACTS + ONE DRAWN · 2026
I
Act I — 2026 Module 01 of 04

DigiGraph

Route any request through the right specialist agent.

A supervisor sub-graph dispatches work to specialists. Tools are discovered by MCP, typed with Pydantic, and orchestrated with LangGraph. Deterministic routing, typed outputs, full traces.

  • LangGraph
  • MCP Registry
  • Pydantic v2
  • OpenAI-compatible
II
Act II — 2026 Module 02 of 04

DigiQuant

Backtest, optimize, and deploy trading strategies.

Three surfaces on one engine. Atlas maps the research. Hermes runs the messenger loop. Kairos strikes the moment. All built on Nautilus, Polars, and typed strategy contracts.

  • NautilusTrader
  • Polars
  • Atlas
  • Hermes
  • Kairos
III
Act III — 2026 Module 03 of 04

DigiSearch

Find the right document. Ground any answer.

Ingest, chunk, embed, retrieve. Multi-mode retrieval with a pluggable vector store registry. Selective indexing by rule, not by default. Citations stitched into every response.

  • Hybrid BM25 + ANN
  • Pluggable stores
  • Citations
IV
Act IV — 2026 Module 04 of 04

DigiChat

Talk to your stack. Ship with your own keys.

A BYOK chat surface that speaks DigiGraph. LiteLLM translates. Auth.js gates access. The UI adapts to the scopes your key carries. Deploy public, private, or embedded.

  • Next.js
  • Auth.js
  • LiteLLM
  • BYOK

Act V — 2026 Interlock · five panels

Plug it in.

Five surfaces, one stack. Choose your interlock.

Plug. Your app, database, and auth bind directly to the modules they need.

MCP. One protocol across every module — tools travel as structured capabilities.

Docker. Every module ships as a container. Laptop, server, cloud — same image.
