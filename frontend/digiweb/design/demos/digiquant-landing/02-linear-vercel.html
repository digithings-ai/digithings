The pipeline

Three agents, one accountable loop from idea to fill.

Signals don't appear from nowhere and orders don't fire blind. Each stage is observable, reproducible, and gated — so you can audit exactly why capital moved.

01RESEARCH

Atlas / researcher

Continuously surveys markets, news and fundamentals, building structured theses with provenance you can trace back to the source.

Polars-native feature store

Stress-tests each thesis against carry, risk budgets and prior deliberations — sizing positions and weighting the book before a single order forms.

Mark-to-market weighting
Rebalancing cadence engine
Thesis → vehicle hierarchy

Infrastructure

Built like a trading desk, shipped like a dev tool.

MCP-first, fully typed, observable end to end. Everything is a discoverable tool, every change traces to an issue, and the whole stack runs where you decide.

NautilusTrader core

A nanosecond-resolution event engine powers backtest, optimize and live — identical code, identical fills, zero rewrite at the production boundary.

Self-hosted, keys in your VPC

BYOK from end to end. No credentials, no positions and no market data ever leave infrastructure you control. Default-free, designed to self-host.

MCP-first capabilities

Every research, deliberation and execution primitive is a discoverable tool — orchestrated by a LangGraph supervisor and callable from any agent.

Walk-forward optimization

Parameter sweeps run as first-class jobs with leakage guards, so the Sharpe you ship in-sample is the Sharpe you actually trade.

Observable by default

Structured logs, correlation IDs and PII redaction across every service. When a fill looks wrong, the trace tells you why in seconds.

Open core, no lock-in

The engine, adapters and agents are open. Plug your own data, broker and models behind clean protocols — we filter, we don't absorb.
