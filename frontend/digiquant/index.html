One pipeline. Three specialists.

Atlas researches. Hermes deliberates. Kairos executes. Every run lands in NautilusTrader — yours to inspect and replay.

01 · research

Atlas

Scheduled research cycles produce structured views — not prose. Outputs persist to a shared store, re-used downstream.

atlas.run_cycle(universe, asof)
02 · signals

Hermes

Translates research into allocations. Every signal timestamped, attributed, and replayable by you, risk, or audit.

hermes.deliberate(views)
03 · execution

Kairos

Executes signals on a NautilusTrader core. Loopback-only by default; live orders stay behind a human gate.

kairos.execute(signals)
