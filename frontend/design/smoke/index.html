Is DigiThings really open source?

Yes — the core stack is MIT-licensed and self-hostable. Every service (orchestration, quant, RAG, chat) runs on your own infrastructure with your own keys.

Do I need to bring my own model keys?

For self-hosting, yes — point the stack at any LiteLLM-supported provider or a local model. A managed option is planned.

Can I use it for live trading?

Backtest and research paths are open. Live-trading paths require explicit configuration and human approval — they are never enabled by default.
