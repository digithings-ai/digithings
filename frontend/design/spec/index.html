// the design canon · v1 · source of truth

Editorial voice.
Terminal truth.

One language for digithings.ai, digiquant.io, Olympus and DigiChat: Fraunces speaks for the humans, Geist Mono testifies for the machines, and every number on every page is real, dated, and auditable. This document is written in the language it defines.

00
// doctrine

Six laws. Everything else is taste.

law 01
Real numbers, dated.

No invented stats, no round marketing numbers, no counters on filler. Every figure carries its provenance: PF 8.72 · as of 2026-06-29. The timestamp is the argument.

law 02
Color is instrumentation.

Emerald means interactive or alive — and it is digiquant's livery, not the stack's. Green means profit, copper means loss — money only. The umbrella is black & white: on digithings.ai the accent IS the ink (--accent: var(--ink), live token). A colored pixel is a gauge reading or a module's flag — never paint.

law 03
Two voices, one page.

Fraunces at weight 400 — never bolded, scale does the shouting — for the human claim. Geist Mono for the machine's testimony. Geist Sans is connective tissue and stays out of the way. The ban binds the serif voice only: sans sub-heads may carry 500–600 as UI weight.

law 04
Hairline flatness.

Depth = one surface step + a 1px hairline. No glass, no glow, no drop shadows on content. The sanctioned luminous set: the footer bloom · one hero moment · the hero scroll-cue · brand-glyph and live-dot micro-glows, small-area only. Everything else stays flat.

law 05
One motion moment.

One entrance per view, then stillness. The flagship page may carry two pinned chapters — pipeline and proof — every other page keeps one at most. All other motion is diegetic — inside terminals and charts, where the product is visibly working. Everything rides one curve.

law 06
Content-first, always.

Every page renders complete without JavaScript. Scripts enhance — they never gate. Reduced-motion collapses every animation to its final state. Skip links, SR descriptions, dialog semantics ship by default.
